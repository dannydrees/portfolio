About

I've spent seven years building marketing and content for companies selling into institutions — public transit agencies, universities, government procurement offices, health systems. The buyers are cautious. The sales cycles are long. The stakes for the people being served are real. That context shapes how I write and how I think about brand.

My background spans product marketing, proposals, editorial content, and brand strategy across B2B SaaS, transit technology, nonprofit associations, and climate. I've built content engines, written white papers that moved enterprise deals, and developed brand guidelines for startups building their identity from scratch.

Before that: APTA, where I worked at the center of federal transit policy and built communications for an industry association representing agencies that move tens of millions of people daily. Government credibility isn't a background detail — it's where I come from.
